FAQ for THE LEGENDZ Community

How To Get Access To The Server

After the welcome screen, you will have to wait for 5-10 minutes ( Please read the rules while you're waiting ) and you will get access to #accept-the-rules. Now, to verify yourself send !verify or react ✅ to the message in the channel. Finally, you've got access to the server !

Server Boosting

You will get a special hoisted role @Server Booster and get access to the Booster Only Channel with permission to change nicknames

Join THE LEGENDZ Clubs

Feel free to join any of THE LEGENDZ's club if you met its requirement.

How To Get Promotion In Clubs

We only give promotion to someone we trust.

How To Become A Moderator

To become a staff member, you must apply through our staff application. The form is available in announcement channel when we require more staff. Moderators are really important to the community, they contribute as much as they could to keep the community safe. Therefore, you should only apply for it if you think you fits the position.

Get Help From Staff Members

Please DM Joe Bot To Report Someone Or Ask For Help.

How To Get [x] Role

See role-info for descriptive information, it shows how any role in the server can be obtained and its permissions.

Why Can’t I Speak [x] Languages In This Server ?

To make things simple, you can only speak/chat in English. Therefore, we don't have to filter every inappropriate or swear words in every languages. For club members, you are allowed to speak in Chinese, English, Tamil And Malay ( In Certain Channel ).

How Can I Suggest New Features For The Server ?

You may suggest anything by DMing our bot @Joe Bot. If our staff team believe it's a good idea they can add it into our private suggestions channel.

How To Add Joe Bot To Your Server ?

Unfortunately this is a privately-owned custom bot for the server.

Can I Use My Alternative Accounts ?

Yes, everyone is allowed to have their alts on the server as long as its not used for entering multiple times in giveaways, or to bypass any sort of punishment another account received.

Proficient Role

Proficient role are given to people we trust. You can get the role if a staff member thinks you fit the role.

Pinging Moderators

Only ping @Moderators when something requires more than 2 Moderators to handle. Instead, you can just ping one of the Moderator.
